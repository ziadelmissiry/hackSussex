Rising Temperatures

As the Earth's temperature rises, it can have a variety of effects, such as more frequent heatwaves, droughts, and wildfires. Warmer temperatures can also lead to changes in ecosystems, such as shifts in plant and animal ranges or the timing of seasonal events like flowering or migration.
